Although each O365 tenant is associated to a specific region (i.e. datacenter) where the tenant’s data will be stored at rest, from a networking perspective Office 365 is a worldwide service with globally distributed endpoints. This is one of reasons why ExpressRoute access for O365 requires the ExpressRoute Premium Add-On, as documented [here](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Azure-ExpressRoute-for-Office-365-6d2534a2-c19c-4a99-be5e-33a0cee5d3bd).
